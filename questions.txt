GENERAL INTERVIEW QUESTIONS
----------------------------
- Tell me about yourself.
- What are your greatest strengths?
- What is your biggest weakness?
- Why do you want to work for our company?
- Where do you see yourself in five years?
- Tell me about a time you faced a challenge at work.
- How do you handle pressure and stressful situations?
- What motivates you?
- Why should we hire you?
- Describe your ideal working environment.
- What are your career goals?
- How do you prioritize your tasks when managing multiple projects?
- Tell me about a time you had a conflict with a colleague. How did you resolve it?
- What makes you unique compared to other candidates?
- How do you handle feedback, especially when it's critical?
- Tell me about a time you failed. What did you learn from it?
- How do you stay organized in your daily work?
- Describe a situation where you had to learn something quickly.
- How do you define success in your career?
- What do you know about our company and why does it interest you?
- What type of leadership style do you respond best to?
- Do you prefer working independently or as part of a team?
- How do you stay motivated during repetitive tasks?
- What would your previous manager or coworkers say about you?
- What is your approach to continuous learning and self-improvement?
- How do you handle tight deadlines?
- What do you do when you don't know how to solve a problem?
- How do you ensure effective communication in a team?
- Tell me about a time when you exceeded expectations at work.
- How do you adapt to changes in the workplace?
- Describe a project you are particularly proud of.
- What steps do you take to ensure quality in your work?
- How do you handle multiple priorities with limited time?
- Describe your decision-making process.
- How do you deal with ambiguity or incomplete information?
- What kind of work environment helps you thrive?
- How do you approach goal setting and tracking your progress?
- Tell me about a time you had to convince someone to see things your way.
- How do you ensure you meet or exceed client expectations?
- What do you do when you're not feeling productive?
- Tell me about a time when you made a mistake and how you handled it.
- How do you balance personal and professional growth?
- What do you believe is the most important soft skill for this role?
- How do you maintain a healthy work-life balance?
- What kind of professional development are you currently pursuing?

TECHNICAL QUESTIONS (DATA SCIENCE & AI)
--------------------------------------
- Can you explain the difference between supervised and unsupervised learning?
- How do you handle missing data in a dataset?
- What is regularization in machine learning and why is it useful?
- How would you explain overfitting to a non-technical stakeholder?
- What is the bias-variance tradeoff?
- Can you describe a machine learning project you've worked on?
- What metrics would you use to evaluate a classification model?
- How do you choose the right algorithm for a specific problem?
- Explain the difference between precision and recall.
- What are the key steps in a data science pipeline?
- What is cross-validation and why is it important?
- How do you ensure your model is interpretable?
- Have you used SHAP or LIME before? What for?
- Explain the concept of a confusion matrix.
- How do you clean and preprocess unstructured data?
